Case notes

Context → Problem → Approach → Artifacts

Policy & systems

United Nations ESCAP — Vietnam SDG4: Evidence-to-Policy Pipeline

Context: Evaluating Vietnam’s pathways toward SDG4 with a focus on equity and learning quality.

Problem: Turn heterogeneous, noisy indicators into actionable priorities without overstating what the data can prove.

Approach: Data harmonization → trend/breakpoint analysis → equity gap curves → scenario planning → implementation translation.

Artifacts: One-page policy brief + technical annex, GIS dashboards, and reproducible methods notes.

Higher-ed platforms

HETERNAL × The University of Sydney — Canvas LMS Augmentation

Problem: AI augmentation must be traceable, auditable, and evaluation-ready in real university workflows.

Approach: Instrumentation-first plugin layer with reproducible RAG/prompting modules and usage guardrails.

Artifacts: Plugin demo, data architecture diagram, and compliance/integration handoff summary.

School-based research

Tsinghua University — MAIC: Real-school quasi-experiment & implementation

Problem: High-stakes interventions require causal evidence, mechanism insight, and fairness monitoring.

Approach: Real-world quasi-experiment design with baseline adjustment, robustness checks, and classroom implementation routines.

Artifacts: Reusable research SOP, analysis framework, and classroom-ready routines for teachers/researchers.

AI classrooms

HETERNAL × Tsinghua University × Haidian pilot — Multimodal learning analytics

Problem: Convert process signals into actionable, calibrated, and fair teacher micro-strategies.

Approach: Collection & alignment protocols → feature engineering → calibrated modeling → fairness monitoring → teacher dashboard + strategy library.

Artifacts: Protocol pack, calibration/fairness checklist, and lightweight teacher dashboard.

Industry evaluation

Yuanfudao — Measurement & causal evaluation for personalization

Problem: Optimize engagement without losing learning outcomes, and avoid misreading correlation as causation.

Approach: Unified event schemas + KPI trees → IRT/BKT calibration → A/B with variance reduction and heterogeneity checks.

Artifacts: KPI tree and guardrail protocol templates, calibration reports, and production dashboard designs.

Course & assessment

Zuoyebang — Backward design + measurement-driven course production

Problem: Content scaling introduces drift: misalignment, inconsistent difficulty, and uneven quality across cohorts.

Approach: Constructive alignment → coverage matrices → error taxonomies → calibration + fairness checks → cohort monitoring.

Artifacts: Coverage matrix, QC rubric, and monitoring dashboard blueprint.

Early math learning

TAL — Pre-K to G3 math: learning progression → interaction design → evidence gates

Problem: AI-assisted content and explanations can drift without constraints, evaluation, and quality control.

Approach: Standards → measurable indicators → interaction specs → instrumentation → calibration + experimental validation.

Artifacts: Curriculum blueprint, item rubrics, experiment and weekly reporting templates.
