Venture & Product Innovation

HETERNAL roadmap

From product → evidence → scale

Phase 1 — Higher Ed (Canvas / USYD)

LMS augmentation with traceable data pipelines, reproducible RAG/prompting modules, and compliance handoff.

Phase 2 — K–12 (AI classroom)

Process signals → teacher decision support → low-burden dashboards and micro-strategy libraries.

Phase 3 — Early Years (3–6)

Assessment-to-training loop with measurement controls, reliability, and implementation feasibility by design.

Product innovation methodology

Building systems that survive real constraints

Research → product translation

From frontline problems → actionable indicators → intervention hypotheses → MVP.

Instrumentation-first

Define event models, data contracts, and metric definitions before building features.

Evidence gates

Key changes must pass experiment/quasi-experiment thresholds (with guardrails).

Implementation & compliance

Consent, privacy impact review, low-bandwidth options, school-side SOPs, rollback-ready releases.

Deployable assets

Reusable building blocks for evidence at scale

Data layer

Data contracts, event schemas, metric definitions, lineage, and replay.

Evaluation layer

Experiment protocols, power templates, robustness & fairness audit checklists.

Decision layer

Teacher dashboards (trends/alerts) and a micro-strategy library (executable and auditable).

Deployment layer

Compliance packs, training SOPs, versioning, and safe rollout/rollback mechanisms.
